Stop 1: Hotel Will Rogers

Address: 524 W. Will Rogers Blvd, Claremore, OK

Today

Historic View

The Hotel Will Rogers has become one of Claremore's most recognizable downtown landmarks. Today it is split into the Will Rogers loft apartments, Gibby's Restaurant, and the French Quarter Coffee Co.

Hotel Will rogers was built between 1929-1930. Visitors once came from across the region to experience Claremore's mineral waters and hospitality. The smell of the sulfur baths would waft though the streets to welcome visitors. Though called radium baths the water did not actuallly contain radium, but rather 13 minerals including sulfur, salt, and iron. The hotel closed in 1991 and was in disrepair. It was about to be demolished, but was purchased by the National Register of Historic Places. In 1997 it was restored for 2.5 million dollars creating 38 one and two bedroom apartments on the upper floors. On the sixth floor three of the famous mineral baths were preserved.
